Plumo Carcinoma

Lung cancer is one of the most common and serious types of cancer. It usually starts in the lungs, but it can also spread to other parts of the body. The main types of lung cancer are non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) and small cell lung cancer (SCLC). Symptoms can include coughing, chest pain, shortness of breath, and weight loss. Early detection and treatment are crucial for improving the chances of recovery. Our prediction tool helps assess the risk of lung cancer based on various health factors.
